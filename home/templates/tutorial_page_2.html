The Slug Molder

{% endblock %} {% block image %} {% endblock %} {% block tutorial_text %}

  • See the litte circles? Drag them around to change the shape of your slug. The shape of the slug controls the shape of the sound. It's a lot like what music technology [people call a sound envelope. The height of the slug determines how loud the sound it, where the slugs head is the start of the sound, an the tail is the end.

  • Push these buttons to change the octave of the slug. This is like how high or low the sound is. A low octave will make a low sound, like a bass or a tuba, whereas a high octave will make a higher sound like a violin or a penny whistle. You will see the antennas move up or down when you change the octave.

  • Click on Save Shape to, you guessed it, saveyour shape, and click the big X in the top corner to close the slugmold box.
