Welcome to SlugJam!

The first slug-based musical sequencer on earth since before the dinosaurs!

Sign Up Now

SlugJam is entirely free, and exists purely for yor enjoyment.

Try it out now by remixing one of the songs that has already been written, or register to get YOUR VERY OWN pallette of individually selected singing slugs to write your own songs and share them with your friends!

And This is just the Start! A whole bunch of new features are being planned, and should be ready soon, including:

  • Slug breeding - Bored of the sound of your current slugs? cross-breed with your friends to get a whole new sound!
  • Longer songs - Imagine being able to arrange seperate loops, end to end, to make complex tracks as long as a train. WOW!
  • Accessories - Decorate your slugs with slug-cessories. From top-hats to mohawks to ball-gowns.
  • World domination - only kidding! Not all creatures from outer-space are interested in such things.
